Autonomous car이란 무엇인가?

A self-driving car, also known as an autonomous vehicle (AV),connected and autonomous vehicle (CAV), full self-driving car or driverless car, or robo-car or robotic car, (automated vehicles and fully automated vehicles in the European Union) is a vehicle that is capable of sensing its environment and moving safely with little or no human input. Self-driving cars combine a variety of sensors to perceive their surroundings, such as radar, lidar, sonar, GPS, odometry and inertial measurement units.[1][5] Advanced control systems interpret sensory information to identify appropriate navigation paths, as well as obstacles and relevant signage.

Connected vehicle platoons[5] and long-distance trucking[9] are seen as being at the forefront of adopting and implementing the technology.
